Pitch

Pip My Dice is a Yahtzee Roguelike experience. Score enough to make it to the next level, customize your dice to score more and collect relics to create unlimited combos! Collect money through the rounds to buy new dice, relics, and customizers to upgrade your dice. Enjoy a different run every time!

Description

Pip My Dice is a roguelike deck builder inspired by Balatro and the Yahtzee game. Customize different dice and use them together with powerful relics to break high scores and climb the leaderboard.

Collect powerful relics to create synergies with your dice and upgrade your combination slots. Take your time and make wise decisions about what you buy in the shop and how you use it. Don't be greedy, you never know if you are going to roll what you are expecting... Unless you customize your dice so much that all faces are the same!

Enjoy endless replayability with the guarantee that every run will be different from the previous one, and save your favorite seeds to replay and share those you like the most.
Customize all faces of your dice individually to make the most of your relics and create game-breaking combos! Rig the odds by modifying the value of the dice faces, and make sure that there is no room for chance. Hold on to precious consumables to get you out of trouble.

History

After getting completely hooked by Balatro during its Next Fest Demo in November 2025, I felt the urge to finally stop making unfinished prototypes and make a game of my own.
I started to design a game with dice inspired by Balatro, while trying my best to create a unique concept and a fun experience.
The goal I set for myself was to sell a copy to a random person and that they would enjoy the game for a couple of hours. I'm very happy that I've now reached that goal even before the game is released, as I have gotten 19/20 positive reviews on the demo with some people playing for dozens of hours!

Features

  • 100+ Relics to create various builds and synergies with your dice
  • 35+ Dice modifiers with unique effects
  • 20+ Bosses to defeat
  • 20+ Consumables to give you some extra help when you need it the most
  • Level up your combination slots as the run goes on
  • Save your favorite seeds to replay and share with your friends
  • Climb the leaderboard with seeded or random runs to compete with the world or your friends!
  • Select from 10+ different starting dice cups
  • Enjoy endless mode to optimize your favorite builds
